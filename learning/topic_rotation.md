### Daily Structure
1. Morning preparation (15 mins)
   - Choose specific topic
   - Gather resources
   - Set learning goals

2. Learning session (1 hour)
   - Active study
   - Note-taking
   - Concept mapping
   - Question formation

3. Evening reflection (15 mins)
   - Knowledge synthesis
   - Connection mapping
   - Next day planning
